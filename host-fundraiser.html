Host a Fundraiser

Make a lasting impact by organizing a fundraiser for Kartavya and supporting underprivileged students.

How to Host a Fundraiser

1. Plan Your Event

Decide the type of fundraiser you want to host, such as a bake sale, walkathon, or online crowdfunding campaign.

2. Spread the Word

Promote your fundraiser through social media, emails, and word of mouth to get as much participation as possible.

3. Host and Collect Donations

Host the event and collect donations. Use platforms like Google Pay or PayPal for seamless transactions.
