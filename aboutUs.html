About Kartavya

Kartavya is a volunteer-driven NGO dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged children. For over 13 years, our efforts have been focused on providing education, moral guidance, and life skills to children who deserve every opportunity to succeed.

Beyond academics, we host workshops, celebrate festivals, and build a nurturing community that fosters confidence, creativity, and self-worth. Our volunteers serve as mentors, friends, and guides, ensuring every child feels loved and supported.

At Kartavya, we believe every child is a future leader, artist, scientist, or dreamer, and it is our responsibility to nurture their potential. Together, we strive for an educated, inclusive, and compassionate society.

Our Milestones

Over the years, Kartavya has achieved significant milestones that inspire us to do more:

13 Years of Service

Providing education, care, and guidance to over 1,000 children since 2012.

Festival Celebrations

Bringing joy and unity through Holi, Diwali, Christmas, and birthday celebrations with our children.

Educational Workshops

Hosting seminars on topics like hygiene, basic etiquettes, and skill development for children.

Community Growth

Inspiring 500+ volunteers to join our mission and contribute to a better future.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: A world where every child has access to quality education, equal opportunities, and a nurturing environment to reach their full potential.

Mission: To empower underprivileged children through education, moral guidance, and skill development while fostering a sense of community and hope.

We aim to touch more lives, inspire change, and create a society where every child has the tools to thrive and succeed.

Join Our Mission
